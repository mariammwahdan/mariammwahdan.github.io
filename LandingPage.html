ABOUT US

Founded in 1920, Banque Misr stands as a pillar of trust and innovation in the Egyptian banking sector. With a rich heritage rooted in supporting the nation's economic growth, we are committed to providing comprehensive financial solutions tailored to meet the evolving needs of our customers. Our expansive network, cutting-edge technology, and dedication to excellence ensure that we remain a trusted partner for individuals, businesses, and communities across Egypt and beyond. At Banque Misr, we take pride in our role in shaping the future of banking while honoring the traditions that define us.
